# Story Assets

## Game Story
CURTIS LOOP: BRICK BLAST — A retro arcade experience.
The player controls a paddle, breaking bricks across infinite levels.
Each level gets faster. Power-ups help or hinder. Survive as long as possible.

## Characters
- **Player Paddle** — The blue bar at the bottom. Moves left/right.
- **The Ball** — Bounces off everything. Size changes with power-ups.
- **Bricks** — 7 rows, colored by row. Top rows take 2 hits on level 2+.
- **Power-Ups** — Colored orbs with symbols. Fall from destroyed bricks.

## Design Notes
- Colorblind mode uses distinct shapes + CBF-safe palette
- Music cycles through 10 retro game styles automatically
- Screen shake on ball loss adds impact feel
- Combo multiplier rewards fast consecutive brick hits
