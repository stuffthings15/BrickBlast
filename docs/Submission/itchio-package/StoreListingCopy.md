## Long Description

**BrickBlast: Velocity Market** is a 2D brick-breaking arcade game designed for short, satisfying
play sessions. Destroy bricks, earn coins, and spend them on a rich marketplace of ball skins,
brick palettes, and themed bonus packs — all persistently tied to your player profile.

### Features

**Complete Gameplay Loop**
Eight handcrafted levels of escalating difficulty, from beginner-friendly rows to fast-moving
mixed grids. Every session offers a clear start, challenge, and reward.

**Five Brick Types**
- Standard Bricks — one hit, clean destruction
- Durable Bricks — multiple hits, visible damage progression
- Reward Bricks — bonus coins on destruction
- Hazard Bricks — punish careless angles with speed penalties
- Moving Bricks — lateral movement adds real-time challenge

**52-Item Marketplace**
Earn coins through gameplay and spend them on:
- 18 ball skins: Fire, Ice, Plasma, Galaxy, Aurora, and more
- 17 brick palettes: Neon, Space, Candy, Sakura, Obsidian, and more
- 17 bonus packs: Each pack re-themes every power-up shape, colour, and icon — from Ninja to
  Dragon Fire to Halloween Horror

**Persistent Player Profiles**
Enter your name at startup and your entire collection — coins, owned items, equipped skins — is
saved and restored automatically. Multiple profiles supported.

**Online Sync**
When connected, your profile is synced to a remote endpoint. Play fully offline and sync when
ready. Sync status is always visible in the HUD.

**Dev Mode**
Power users can unlock everything instantly for testing and demo purposes.

**Audio Feedback**
Procedurally synthesised sound effects and MIDI music provide satisfying audio on every action.

**Colorblind Mode**
Power-up icons include text labels in colorblind mode for full accessibility.

### Controls
- **Mouse / Touch:** drag to move paddle, click to launch
- **Keyboard:** arrow keys + Space + Esc
- **Gamepad:** XInput supported (left stick, A button, Start)

### System Requirements
- Windows 10 or later
- .NET 10 runtime
- 50 MB disk space
